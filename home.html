Weatherbot


Simple. Quick. Amazing.

Finally a bot dedicated to weather! Very simple and easy to use commands, getting started is as easy as 1, 2, 3

We even do stuff automaticly! So you never have to check the weather again, the bot does it for you!!

Now
w!now location

Grabs the most up to date weather for the location. Fairly fast but give it a few seconds to do cool things.
Can give weather anywhere on the earth, just be careful with a few places but it should work.
Everyday
w!everyday locationName

Gets the weather 24 hours from when you set it. This means you can get the weather in the morning
and be ready to do whatever you want to do knowing weatherbot has your back.
